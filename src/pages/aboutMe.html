About The Planner


Jessica Cabral

Mississauga, Ontario

Work Experience: Associate Wedding and Event Coordinator at  Serendipity & Co. Inc. from 2011-2014

Services: Full wedding planning, partial wedding planning, month of wedding coordination, baptisms, wedding showers, baby showers, birthdays and fundraisers

"Close your eyes and tell me what you see..." The Line I swear by as an Event Coordinator.

My name is Jessica Cabral, a young, creative and eye for detailed individual whose passion is to make peoples visions become a reality.

From grade eight graduation to my best friends wedding I have always had an interest in the event world. Throughout high school I found myself volunteering and getting involved in student organizations such as the student council, dance club, yearbook and prom committee. Outside of school I continued to volunteer my time in the Portuguese Community planning events and fundraisers with a local youth group.

I was then introduced to a company called Serendipity & Co. Inc. where I became an intern for six months. My experience working with Serendipity & Co. Inc. has allowed me to fully acknowledge my passion for wedding planning.

Every wedding becomes personal and special to me. I have become extremely passionate for every wedding I have been a part of. My personality truly encompasses the world of event coordination; I enjoy the thrill, busy nights and the new ways of learning the industry. Whether it's bringing the dreams and visions of brides to life, creating a special surprise party, celebrating the anniversary of love, or a fundraiser, I bring forth everything I have in making every event unique and special to my clients.

As I like to say, “Your special day should be stress free and filled with joy and happiness.” I can ensure that every step with me will be guaranteed to be a smooth, momentous, and memorable experience for you and your family
